Our Journey

The Story of Maa Gaura

2004

The Seed is Sown

Founded with just 12 students in a rented villa, the vision was simple: to bring authentic Montessori education to the heart of Hamirpur, focusing on the child's innate desire to learn.

2012

Growing Roots

As the community's trust grew, we moved to our permanent campus. We expanded our curriculum up to Class 5, integrating local cultural heritage with global pedagogical standards.

2024

The Modern Legacy

Now serving students up to Class 8, Goura City Montessori stands as a beacon of grounded, holistic education, known for producing self-reliant and compassionate citizens.

A Message from our Principal

"Education is not what the teacher gives; education is a natural process spontaneously carried out by the human individual, and is acquired not by listening to words but by experiences upon the environment."

At Goura City Montessori, we don't just teach subjects; we nurture the spirit. Our commitment to the Montessori method ensures that your child grows into a confident, independent thinker who is deeply connected to their roots here in Hamirpur. We invite you to be a part of this beautiful journey of discovery.

Mrs. Sarita Sharma

Founder & Principal, M.Ed (Montessori Specialist)
